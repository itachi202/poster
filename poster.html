Handicrafts of Sikkim




Wood Carving


Wood carving is an ancient art form of both
the Lepchas and the Bhutias of Sikkim.
The main raw material used in this craft is wood.









Handloom Weaving







Lepcha weaves or 'thara' is woven in vertical looms with a backstrap.
Such looms are of small width. Traditional design with different
colours are used to make tharas which are used for making bedspreads, belts,
curtains, cushion covers, table mats etc, apart from their traditional dress.
